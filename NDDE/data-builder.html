NDDE Custom Data Builder

Welcome to the NDDE Custom Data Builder, a specialized interface dedicated to creating custom datasets for visualizing natural disasters. This intuitive and user-friendly page is designed to streamline the process of compiling and structuring data related to various types of natural disasters, such as droughts, earthquakes, floods, and more. You can download your file after inputing and visualize with our visualizer tool on the Homepage for free!
